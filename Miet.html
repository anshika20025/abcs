The Bachelor of Technology degree is a professional engineering undergraduate degree awarded after culmination of four years of engineering study and research. The degree course follows the standard curriculum predetermined by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and the University Grants Commission (India) (UGC).

Academic Eligibility Criteria for B.Tech Course

The applicant should have completed his higher secondary education with Physics, Chemistry & Mathematics. The applicant is required to appear for various entrance exams to secure admission. In numerous entrance exam it is important for the applicant to secure at least 60% marks in the above subjects which varies from exam to exam.

Eligibility Criteria for Foreign Students for B.Tech Course

These applicant should have completed the course equivalent to the 12th standard. These include IGCSE, CBSE, A level etc. Also these applicants should have studied the subjects mentioned for the residents of the country. They should have scored a minimum of 60% in these subjects.
